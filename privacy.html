Privacy Policy

App: Letter Clash
Developer: Glitch Yard
Effective Date: April 6, 2026
Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Summary: Letter Clash is a real-time PvP word game. We collect gameplay data, device identifiers, and account information to run the game. We use Firebase, PlayFab, and LevelPlay (ironSource) for core functionality. You can opt out of analytics and personalized ads at any time in Settings → Privacy.

1. Who We Are

Letter Clash ("the Game", "we", "us", "our") is developed and operated by Glitch Yard. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@glitchyard.studio.

This policy applies to the Letter Clash mobile application available on iOS (Apple App Store) and Android (Google Play Store), including all game features, services, and any related websites.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Anonymous accounts (default): A randomly generated user ID. No personal information required.
  • Apple Sign-In: Your Apple-provided user ID and, if you choose, your name and email address.
  • Google Sign-In: Your Google user ID, display name, email address, and profile photo URL.
  • Facebook Login: Your Facebook user ID, display name, and profile photo URL.
  • Display name: A username you choose, shown on leaderboards and to other players.

Anonymous accounts can be upgraded to a persistent account at any time. Your game progress transfers automatically.

2.2 Gameplay Data

We collect data about how you play the game to operate leaderboards, matchmaking, and game features:

  • Match results, scores, win/loss records
  • Words submitted and performance statistics
  • Level progression, trophy count, and rank tier
  • In-game currency earned and spent
  • Power-up and booster usage
  • Daily streak and login history

2.3 Device Information

We and our service providers automatically collect:

  • Device type, operating system version, and platform (iOS/Android)
  • App version
  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) — only with your consent
  • IP address (used to estimate country; not stored long-term)
  • Session timestamps and session duration

2.4 Purchase Information

If you make in-app purchases, Apple or Google processes the payment. We receive:

  • A transaction ID and receipt for verification
  • The product ID purchased (e.g., "clash_pass")
  • Timestamp of purchase

We do not receive or store your credit card number, billing address, or full payment details. All payments are handled by Apple or Google.

2.5 Analytics Events

With your consent, we collect analytics events to improve the game. Examples include:

  • App launch, session start and end
  • Tutorial steps completed or skipped
  • Screens viewed, buttons tapped
  • Match started and match completed
  • Ad shown, rewarded ad completed
  • Purchase started or completed
  • Errors and crashes

You can disable analytics collection in Settings → Privacy → Analytics.

2.6 Crash Reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports. These include the device model, OS version, app version, and a stack trace of what caused the crash. Crash reports help us identify and fix bugs. This data does not include your personal information or gameplay content.

2.7 Push Notification Token

If you allow push notifications, we receive a device token from Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) and store it on our servers to send you notifications such as match results, daily rewards, and game updates. You can revoke notification permission at any time in your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy. By using the Game, you acknowledge that your data will be processed by these providers.

4.1 Firebase (Google)

We use several Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages user sign-in (anonymous, Apple, Google, Facebook) and account security
  • Firebase Analytics — collects gameplay and engagement events (consent-gated)
  • Firebase Crashlytics — records app crashes and diagnostic data
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — delivers push notifications

Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 PlayFab (Microsoft)

PlayFab is our backend game service. It stores your player profile, game progress, leaderboard scores, and matchmaking data on Microsoft Azure servers.

Privacy policy: privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

4.3 LevelPlay / ironSource (Unity Technologies)

We use LevelPlay (powered by ironSource) to serve advertisements — specifically rewarded video ads and interstitials. LevelPlay may collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA/GAID) — only with your consent
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, completions)
  • Device type, OS version, IP-derived country

On iOS, you will be shown Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before any advertising identifier is accessed. If you decline, only non-personalized (contextual) ads will be shown.

You can change your personalized ads preference at any time in Settings → Privacy → Personalized Ads.

Privacy policy: ironSource Privacy Policy

4.4 Apple App Store / Google Play (In-App Purchases)

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple or Google. We use Unity Purchasing as the client-side integration, but payment data never touches our servers.

  • Apple: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We share data only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers: Firebase, PlayFab, and LevelPlay as described in Section 4. These providers are contractually bound to process data only as directed by us.
  • Other players: Your display name and trophy count are visible on leaderboards. Opponent display names are visible during matches.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.
  • Business transfers: If Glitch Yard is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you in advance.

6. Advertising

Letter Clash shows advertisements to support free gameplay. We use rewarded video ads (you choose to watch them for in-game rewards) and interstitial ads (shown between matches). We do not show banner ads.

Personalized Ads

With your consent, ads may be personalized based on your interests and device activity. You can opt out of personalized ads at any time in Settings → Privacy → Personalized Ads. After opting out, you will still see ads, but they will be contextual only.

iOS App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14+, you will be shown Apple's ATT prompt before any advertising identifier is accessed. Tapping "Allow Tracking" enables personalized ads. Tapping "Ask App Not to Track" limits ad personalization.

7. Children's Privacy

Letter Clash is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages.

If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at privacy@glitchyard.studio and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Privacy Rights

All Users

  • Opt out of analytics: Settings → Privacy → Analytics toggle
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Settings → Privacy → Personalized Ads toggle
  • Manage push notifications: Your device's notification settings
  • Delete your account: Settings → Account → Delete Account — removes all your data from our servers
  • Request data deletion by email: Send a request to privacy@glitchyard.studio with subject "Data Deletion Request"

European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you also have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request we limit processing of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for analytics or personalized ads at any time

To exercise these rights, email privacy@glitchyard.studio. We will respond within 30 days.

California (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Delete personal information (via in-app account deletion or email request)
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information: Glitch Yard does not sell or share your personal information as defined under the CCPA/CPRA. We do not exchange personal information for monetary or other valuable consideration. If you believe your data has been sold or shared without consent, contact us at privacy@glitchyard.studio with the subject line "Do Not Sell My Info" and we will investigate promptly.

9. Data Retention

  • Active account data: Retained for as long as your account exists
  • Deleted account data: Removed from our servers within 30 days of your deletion request
  • Analytics events: Firebase retains raw event data for up to 2 months; aggregated reports may be retained longer
  • Crash logs: Retained for 90 days
  • Inactive accounts: Accounts with no activity for 24 months may be deleted after prior notice

10. Data Security

We use TLS encryption for all data in transit. Player data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud) and PlayFab (Microsoft Azure), both of which maintain industry-standard security practices. Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel.

No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. Firebase and PlayFab maintain appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for transfers from the EEA.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and show an in-app notification on your next session. For significant changes, we will re-present the consent dialog so you can review and re-accept.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or to request account/data deletion:

  • Email: privacy@glitchyard.studio
